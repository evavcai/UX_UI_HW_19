About Me

Hello! My name is Eva.

I’m a UX designer based in Austin, TX. As an undergraduate at Rice University majoring in cognitive science and psychology, I was taught to extract qualitative insights from large amounts of quantitative data to understand human cognition. I want to utilize my knowledge of human thought processes to identify individual pain points and develop solutions that can change people’s lives.

After interning for a decentralized finance startup company in 2021, I realized that the world’s future financial system lies in blockchain technologies. My number one priority for designing on a system that many are skeptical about is to convey a sense of trust. Through using a user-centered design approach, my goal is to generate solutions that empower users to feel confident about their financial decisions.

I truly believe research and design can bridge the gap between everyday users and seemingly complex blockchain technologies.

My UX Skills

User Research

User research is the most important step in the design process. I always begin a project with thorough interviews and data collection in order to identify the problem that needs solving.

User Interface Design

Once a solution is made, I begin to sketch, wireframe, and prototype the interface. The tools I use are Figma, Adobe, InVision, and Visual Studio Code.

Usability Testing

Iteration of the design only improves the outcome. I believe conducting in-depth usability tests is required to complete a project.
